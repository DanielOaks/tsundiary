tsundiary is a place to record your life.

Every day, you get a blank entry that looks like this. You can write whatever you want in it.

Every night, at 4 a.m., the previous day's entry is set in stone and you get a new one.

You are also shown your old entries at certain time intervals, like a week, a month, and a year.
